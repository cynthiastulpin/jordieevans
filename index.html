About Me

🐑

As Admin and Web Director at Funfords Mini Farm, I'm dedicated to supporting our mission of rescuing and caring for abandoned and unwanted animals. I coordinate our outreach efforts, manage our digital presence, and work with our amazing team of volunteers and freelancers to ensure every animal gets the care and love they deserve.

⚠️ Before you visit: If you have found an abandoned or neglected animal, or need to surrender your pet, please contact me first. We need to determine if we have the space and funds to accept any animal, so it's important that we know in advance before any animals show up. We do our best to accommodate every animal that needs our help.

About Funfords

🐷

"We are a family friendly mini farm who rescue unwanted animals of all shapes and sizes with a view of rehabilitation and to provide immersive farm experiences for everyone. We rely 100% on donations to keep our farm going and to provide their second chances for a forever home."
